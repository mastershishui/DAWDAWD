Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our app

What is Pinoy Translator?

Pinoy Translator is a comprehensive language tool designed for Filipinos and anyone who wants to connect deeper with the Philippines. It translates English into 10 local Philippine languages and vice versa with cultural accuracy.

Which languages are supported?

We support 10 major Philippine languages: Tagalog, Bisaya, Ilocano, Hiligaynon, Kapampangan, Bicolano, Waray, Pangasinan, Maguindanao, and Tausug.

Do I need the internet to use Pinoy Translator?

Yes, Pinoy Translator requires an internet connection to access our translation database and provide accurate translations in real-time.

Is Pinoy Translator free?

Yes! Pinoy Translator is completely free to use. We believe in making language translation accessible to everyone.

Can I use voice input and output?

Yes! Pinoy Translator supports both voice input for speaking your text and voice output for listening to translations.
